About Us

Nikita Mehra, Director of OneSource Immigration Inc. is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) and a member in good Standing with ICCRC (Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council). She provides personalized consulting services to all her clients regarding the optimal immigration option available according to their profile and personal goals. She moved to Canada from India and is the perfect contact point to advise you about the whole Canada Immigration process as she has gone through the whole process herself and started a new life with family here. She is fluent in English, Hindi, Punjabi and Urdu languages.
