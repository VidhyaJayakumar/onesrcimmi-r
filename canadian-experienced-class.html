Canada Experienced Class Program

To be eligible to apply under Canadian Experience Class- CEC Program, a temporary foreign workers must meet the following requirements:

  • atleast one year of full-time or equivalent of work within the last 3 years before the date of applying under the National Occupational Classification (NOC) code 0,A or B;
  • language proficiency results equivalent to Canadian Language Benchmark (CLB) 7 for NOC 0 or A skill level or (CLB) 5 for NOC B skill level.
