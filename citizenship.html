Citizenship

Becoming a Canadian Citizen brings many advantages. Canadian citizenship is managed by IRCC & it allows a person to apply for if it meets the following conditions:

  • you are a permanent resident
  • you have lived in Canada for 3 out of the last 5 years
  • you have filed your taxes,
  • you pass a test on your rights, responsibilities and knowledge of Canada
  • you must prove your language skills
  • there are other conditions that may apply

We will assist you with personalized step-by-step procedure to successfully complete and submit your citzenship application with the IRCC.
