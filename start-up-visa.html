Startup Visa

This program is designed for individuals who wants to grow their business in Canada. Eligible applicants get full support to open and run their business from their supporting business associations in Canada . In order to be eligible, you must get letter of support from approved business group who is supporting the business idea. The other eligibility requirements for this program are that you:

  • must meet minimum language requirements in English or French;
  • must have a letter of support from a designated organization;
  • must clear security and medical examination; and
  • must bring sufficient funds to settle;
