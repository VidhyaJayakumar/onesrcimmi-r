Canada Express Entry Program

Express Entry was launched in 2015 that makes the immigration process fast. Before this, applicants were selected on the basis of first come, first serve basis but now in this new system, they are picked up on the basis of Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) ranks. Express Entry is not an immigration program rather it is a fast track processing system which selects skilled workers under Economic Classes. It targets and issue Invitation to Apply (ITA) to applicants that have the ability to contribute to economic growth and prosperity of Canada.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

Potential candidates should be qualified under one of the accompanying government monetary movement programs.

  • The Federal Skilled Worker Class (FSWC)
  • The Canadian Experience Class (CEC)
  • The Federal Skilled Trades Class (FSTC)
