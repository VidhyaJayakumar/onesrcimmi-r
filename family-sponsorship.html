Canada - Parents & Grandparents Program

Parents and Grandparents are the torchbearers of thoughts and beliefs in every family.Goes without saying, grandparents are very important in our lives, when it comes to imbibing great values in our kids.That's why Parents and Grandparents Program is important too.

This Program is to sponsor Parents and Grandparents of Permanent Residents & Canadian Citizens & they receive Permanent Residency and eventually Citizenship once approved under this program.

  • be a Canadian citizen, permanent resident of Canada;
  • be 18 years of age or older;
  • be residing in Canada;
  • meet the minimum required income for this program and provide proof of income to IRCC by submitting notices of assessment issued by the Canadian Revenue Agency (CRA) for the last 3 taxation years before you apply; and
  • Sign an undertaking
    • to financially support the sponsored for 20 years (starting when they become permanent residents); and
    • to repay any social assistance benefits paid to the sponsored family members (if applicable) for a period of 20 years.
    • If the sponsor resides in Quebec, an additional "undertaking" must be signed with the province of Quebec.
