Study In Canada

Canada provides excellent opportunity to boost your prospects for an interesting career because of its Advanced Quality Education.This is one of the major factors in attracting international students from around the globe as it helps in rewarding one's career and they choose to make Canada as their Home. It is because of the multiculturalism of this country that you feel welcomed as it embraces diversity.

Study permit is not a visa.The study permit is a document issued by IRCC that allows International Students to study at designated learning institutions in Canada.Interested internationals students need to apply to IRCC for study permit once they receive letter of acceptance from their university or college in Canada. Also you have the option to work part -time during your studies & bring your family here, and the educational qualification & work experience you gain may help facilitate an application for Canadian permanent residence.
